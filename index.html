Frequently Asked Questions

  • The MetaMask wallet is a self-custodial crypto wallet app that gives users secure access to blockchain applications, as well as their digital identities and assets. Users can interact with decentralized applications (dapps) on web3, manage their cryptocurrency portfolio, mint NFTs, and much more. Also available as a browser extension and a mobile app, MetaMask offers features including secure login, key vaults, and a token wallet.
  • To use the MetaMask app on mobile, follow these steps:
    Warning: Do not download MetaMask from unofficial sources:
    • Only download MetaMask from the official app store appropriate to your device/OS.
    • Avoid cloned versions programmed to record your Secret Recovery Phrase.
    
Step 1: Download MetaMask wallet on iOS or Android:
    • Open the App Store and hit the search button in the bottom right.
    • Input "MetaMask" into the search bar.
    • Be VERY CAREFUL that you don't install a fake MetaMask app! Look for the one with 50K+ ratings or 10M+ downloads.
    • Install MetaMask iOS or Android. Tap 'Install' and wait for the process to finish.
    
Step 2: Set up your MetaMask wallet:
    • Once installed, open the app and follow the prompts to create your wallet.
    • Back up your Secret Recovery Phrase somewhere safe and offline.

    Step 3: Fund your MetaMask wallet:
    • After creating your wallet, you'll see a prompt to fund it.
    • You can buy crypto with a debit card or credit card, receive crypto from another account, or transfer crypto from a CEX (Central Exchange).
    Learn how to use your MetaMask wallet with our Getting Started with MetaMask guide.
